Interactive prototype module

3D Solar Neighbourhood Map

Rotatable Three.js atlas of the local Galactic volume. Open clusters are shown as stellar populations with bright named members. The data layer is prepared for the 400 pc thesis catalogue: bright stars, open clusters, molecular clouds and dust surfaces exported from the Python pipeline. Click a cluster label to orbit around it. Molecular clouds and H5 dust are rendered as softer volumetric 3D structures for clarity and speed.

Data references
